The One Percent Company

Anti Capitalism

Here's an idea.

The whole premise of companies and new-age startups relies on possibilities of getting funded by financial institutions and individuals at any or many stages of their growth and development.

This has been an age-long practice and applies in many fields of work, including and not limited to engineering, medicine, technology, etc. The model has proven to be successful over the years, and has given us great businesses and marvelous institutions.

Great business models also build great billionaires and millionaires. And rightly so, because they deserve it owing to all their hard work and perseverence. These enterpreneurs and venture capitalists shape the world with their far-fetched vision, unique observability and ability to solve problems at scale.

Here's the problem though.
